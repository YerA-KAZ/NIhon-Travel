Cosplay · Conventions · 2025–2026

WHERE & WHEN
TO COSPLAY

Cosplay culture in Japan comes alive at massive conventions like Comic Market (Comiket) and dedicated cosplay festivals held across Tokyo and beyond. The most iconic venue is Tokyo Big Sight in Odaiba, where hundreds of thousands of fans gather to celebrate anime, manga, and creativity. From indoor exhibition halls to outdoor cosplay areas, these events transform entire districts into living anime worlds. You’ll see everything from screen-perfect recreations to original character designs, all brought together by a passionate community. Whether it’s Comiket’s legendary scale or smaller cosplay-focused festivals in places like Ikebukuro and Akihabara, Japan offers an unmatched experience for anyone who wants to not just watch anime — but become a part of it.
